Monetisation of AI-Driven Connectivity
Telecom operators have invested hundreds of billions in 5G infrastructure. CAPEX is declining but returns are not yet following. The next monetisation layer is not another connectivity tier - it is AI inference delivered over that connectivity as a managed service.

Enterprises need sovereign AI without building private GPU infrastructure. Telcos already own the network, the SLA framework, and the enterprise relationships. AI MoDaaS turns those assets into a new billable product: AI inference connectivity billed per token, governed end-to-end, and delivered autonomously.

Sovereign AI and Governance Requirements
Data residency, regulatory compliance, and geopolitical risk are reshaping enterprise AI procurement. Enterprises in GCC, EU, and regulated markets cannot route sensitive AI workloads through foreign hyperscaler infrastructure.

Telecom operators are uniquely positioned as trusted, national infrastructure providers - operating within regulatory frameworks their enterprise clients already depend on. AI MoDaaS adds a governance layer - TMF915 sovereign compliance, deterministic rule enforcement, cryptographic proof of sovereignty - that hyperscalers structurally cannot provide.

Agentic Governance Architecture

Neuro SAN - Why It Matters

Cognizant Neuro SAN is an open-source multi-agent AI accelerator. AI MoDaaS is one of the first live multi-vendor Catalyst implementations built on it - replacing ServiceNow with a sovereign-first, standards-native agentic architecture.

Customer Agent
Intent Capture & Order Submission
  • Captures all service parameters from a single natural language or voice input - no sequential form filling
  • Handles text and voice interchangeably - same agent, same intelligence, same output
  • Supports multi-intent ordering - customer chains multiple service orders in one session
  • Generates fully compliant TMF921 intent documents and submits directly to BSS
  • Sensitive credentials are handled securely - never exposed in the conversation stream
  • Multi-turn memory maintained natively across the full conversation
NAI Governance Agent
Sovereign Compliance & Audit
  • Enforces all five TMF915 sovereignty rules through deterministic logic - no AI model in the governance decision path
  • Generates a cryptographic sovereignty credential on every approved intent
  • Writes an immutable audit entry to the graph database on every governance decision
  • Runs as an architecturally isolated process - independent of the Customer Agent
  • LLM-agnostic by design - the underlying AI model can be swapped without changes to governance logic
  • Audit trail is regulator-ready which is timestamped, tamper-evident, and fully queryable
